# FOSDEM 2008 pictures

![A group of people attentively listening in the Drupal developer room at FOSDEM 2008.](http://default/files/cache/fosdem-2008/fosdem-1-640w.jpg)

I spent a lot of time talking in the conference hallways at [FOSDEM](http://fosdem.org) last Sunday, so I only took a handful of pictures of what went on in the Drupal Developer Room.

![A group of people attentively listening in the Drupal developer room at FOSDEM 2008.](http://default/files/cache/fosdem-2008/fosdem-1-640w.jpg)
*Some of the people in the Drupal developer room at FOSDEM.*
![A presenter explains new features in Drupal 6, pointing at a slide about the theme developer module.](http://default/files/cache/fosdem-2008/fosdem-2-640w.jpg)
*Gábor explaining all the <a href="http://drupal.org/drupal-6.0">new features in Drupal 6</a>. On this slide, he is talking about the <a href="http://ftp.drupal.org/files/videocasts/moshe-theme-developer.mov">awesome theme developer module</a> \(QuickTime movie\) that was made possible by Drupal 6's theme system improvements.*
![A speaker gives a presentation about the Solr module for Drupal at FOSDEM 2008, standing in front of a chalkboard.](http://default/files/cache/fosdem-2008/fosdem-3-640w.jpg)
*Robert gave a great presentation about the <a href="http://drupal.org/project/apachesolr">Solr module for Drupal</a>.*
