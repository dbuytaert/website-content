[Boekenbeurs.be](http://boekenbeurs.be), the site of Belgian's famous book fair, received 3 awards at the [Belgian CMS Awards](http://www.cms-awards.be/) last week. The site, built with [Drupal](https://www.drupal.org), was designed and implemented by [Calibrate](http://calibrate.be). The site received an award for web 2.0 functionality, SEO implementation and best public overall award. *Stunning design!*
