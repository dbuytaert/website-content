As [reported in the New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2009/09/02/business/media/02public.html?_r=2), [NowPublic](http://nowpublic.com), a citizen journalism website built on [Drupal](https://www.drupal.org), was acquired by [Examiner.com](http://examiner.com). Soon, one of the top-100 websites in the world will be running Drupal!*Congratulations to the NowPublic team!*

*(Disclosure: I am an advisor to NowPublic.)*
