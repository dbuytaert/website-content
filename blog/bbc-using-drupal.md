# BBC using Drupal

[BBC Magazines](http://www.immediate.co.uk/) is the magazine publishing division of the [British Broadcasting Corporation](https://www.bbc.co.uk/) (BBC). Turns out that they have a number of magazine websites built on [Drupal](https://www.drupal.org):

- [BBC Music Magazine](http://www.bbcmusicmagazine.com)
- [BBC Countryfile Magazine](http://www.bbccountryfilemagazine.com/)
- [BBC Focus Magazine](http://www.sciencefocus.com/)
- [BBC Who Do You Think You Are Magazine](http://www.bbcwhodoyouthinkyouaremagazine.com/)
- [BBC History Magazine](http://www.bbchistorymagazine.com/)
- [BBC Home And Antiques](http://www.bbchomesandantiques.com/)
