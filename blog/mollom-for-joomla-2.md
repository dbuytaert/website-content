# Mollom for Joomla

Right on the heals of the [Wordpress plugin for Mollom](https://dri.es/mollom-for-wordpress), [Markus Bopp](http://www.crosstec.de) got [his Joomla extension for Mollom](https://extensions.joomla.org/component/option,com_mtree/task,viewlink/link_id,5297/Itemid,35/) accepted into [the official Joomla Extension Directory](http://extensions.joomla.org). It is great to see Mollom being adopted by more and more platforms. *Thanks Markus!*
