We're currently determining what products to develop, how to build them, and who to sell them to. Soon, we'll be identifying real customers to validate our product ideas. We're also looking to [grow the team](https://www.acquia.com/jobs) so we can start developing our first products.

At the same time, we're working with the [Drupal community](https://www.drupal.org) to advance the Drupal project: Gabor is working feverishly on the Drupal 6 release; Jay, Jeff and Kieran are part of the [DrupalCon Boston team](https://dri.es/drupalcon-boston) and myself, I'm spearheading the election of the [Drupal Association's next Board of Directors](https://dri.es/drupal-association-organization-chart-2008-wishlist), helping with the Drupal 6 release, and more.

We're only getting started, but it has already been quite a ride so far ...
