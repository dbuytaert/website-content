# Hagen's Drupal 6 book in the mail

Hagen Graf has written his second German Drupal book: [Drupal 6: Websites entwickeln und verwalten mit dem Open Source-CMS](http://www.addison-wesley.de/main/main.asp?page=deutsch/bookdetails&ProductID=131866&piacode=&pszielgruppe=&SID=%7B1680E447-D43F-4F86-8397-576FB3BEA93A%7D&TOKEN=%7B8EE5E5BF-5D70-44D7-9EFC-8861498A8B6E%7D) published by [Addison-Wesley](http://www.addison-wesley.de). The book also comes with a German Drupal 6 training video. And for every copy sold, 1 EUR is donated to the [Drupal Association](https://associaiton.drupal.org/). *Thanks Hagen!*
