Randstad, a company providing temporary and permanent employment HR services, is using [Drupal](https://www.drupal.org) for their French site: <http://randstad.fr>. Randstad is the second largest HR services provider in the world and has major positions in over 50 countries. Randstad is listed on the Euronext Amsterdam stock exchange.
